# ISB Certificate Programme in Leadership with AI
## Pre-programme Learner Expectations & Legal Name Survey

---

### [Quiz] Pre-Programme Survey (11 questions, survey)

A ~5-minute survey to learn about the participant, their reasons for taking the programme, and their expectations.

### [Quiz] Test Your Understanding of Plagiarism (8 questions, practice quiz)

**Declaration of Understanding and Consent** — By submitting the quiz, the participant acknowledges agreement with the academic integrity policy, understands that plagiarism and uncited copying are violations, and accepts responsibility for the originality of submissions. The activity is compulsory but carries no points.

### [Quiz] Student Code of Conduct and Programme Agreement (1 question, survey)

Participants are asked to read and acknowledge the agreement:
- Respect the privacy of other participants.
- Respect diversity of opinions and cultures.
- Use a single account and provide correct information.
- Do not reproduce programme/participant content unless allowed under copyright terms.
- Do not submit others' work as your own or act to improve/harm others' points.

**Submissions:** All assignments must be completed by Wednesday, August 26, 2026 to receive a certificate. On-time submissions marked complete within one week; late submissions marked before programme end. No submissions accepted after August 26, 2026.

**Academic Integrity:** Participants are individually accountable for submitted work; plagiarism is academic dishonesty.

**Refund Policy:**
- Cancellation/withdrawal notice must be in writing 14+ days before the programme start date for a full refund (excluding non-refundable application fee).
- Requests less than 14 days before start: refund minus a processing fee of INR 5,000 plus GST.
- No refunds after programme commencement (exception: IITD certificate programs — refunds within 15 days of start, 80% of total fee, taxes non-refundable).
- If the programme is cancelled, full refund including taxes, no penalty.
- If rescheduling delays commencement by more than one month, full refund of academic fees plus taxes. No refund for rescheduling delays under one month.
- Refund requests to Support.India@emeritus.org.

**Deferment Policy:** A one-time deferral to the next available cohort may be requested within 30 days of programme commencement, subject to batch availability, a valid request with supporting documents, and payment of pending fees. Non-refundable deferral fee: Rs 10,000 + taxes (certificate courses) or Rs 20,000 + taxes (senior executive programmes), plus any fee differential. Payment due within 5 business days of deferral approval. Deferral only considered while the current cohort is ongoing.

### [Page] System Requirements and Tools

Minimum system requirements:
- Disk space: at least 10.0 GB.
- Desktop OS: Windows 7 or later, macOS, or Linux.
- Mobile OS: Android or iOS.
- Devices: laptop, desktop or mobile with webcam and microphone.
- Browser (preferred order): Chrome, Firefox, Safari.
- High-speed internet, 1.5 MB/s or better.
- Headphones/earphones recommended.
