# Module 1: AI and ML — An Overview (Week 1)

> This week: 9 videos, 1 optional discussion, 1 required assignment (~4–5 hours).

---

### [Page] Module 1: Introductions and Instructions

Prof. Anand takes learners through the world of AI — its evolution from concept to cutting-edge technology, the different types of AI and their applications across business functions, the driving forces behind AI's rapid growth, and why embracing AI is becoming crucial for businesses.

**Programme-level outcomes addressed this week:**
- Demonstrate a deep conceptual understanding of AI and its implications for business performance.
- Analyse real-world case studies to identify successful AI initiatives and their impact on business operations.

**Week learning outcomes:**
- Explore AI and the primary factors contributing to its growth.
- Analyse the application and use of various AI methods within an organisation.
- Evaluate how AI can be utilised in managerial functions.

**Key activities:** Videos 1–9; Required Assignment 1.2: Applications of AI. All to be completed by Wednesday, April 15, 2026, 11:59 IST.

**Self-study:** Optional Discussion 1.1: Shaping the Future with AI.

### [Assignment] Required Assignment 1.2: Applications of AI

**Points:** 25 | **Due:** April 15, 2026 (11:59 PM IST) | **Submission:** online upload | **Average completion time:** 90 minutes

**Learning outcomes addressed:**
- Analyse the application and use of various AI methods within an organization.
- Evaluate how AI can be utilised in managerial functions.

**Instructions:** Leverage knowledge from the module and answer the following questions with detailed, analytical responses drawing on module concepts, theories, and frameworks.

**Questions:**
1. **AI and competitive advantage** — Describe how you can use AI to enhance your company's competitive advantage. Think beyond automation: which new opportunities can it unearth and what challenges will it involve? How will those initiatives align with your company's key strengths? Which new capabilities (technological and managerial) do you need to acquire short- and long-term?
2. **Aligning AI to strategy and organization** — Describe how you would align AI initiatives with the overall strategic goals of your organization. Provide a specific example.
3. **Leadership and AI advocacy** — As a senior leader, how would you advocate for AI adoption within your organization? Discuss the key steps to promote AI-friendly policies and practices.
4. **Creating a healthy data and AI culture** — Explain the importance of creating a healthy data and AI culture. What strategies would you employ to foster such a culture?
5. **Scaling AI in organizations** — Discuss the challenges and best practices for scaling AI. Provide an example of a successful AI scaling initiative and what made it effective.

**Submission:** Upload a Word document with your responses → Start Assignment → upload file → Submit Assignment.
