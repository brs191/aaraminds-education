## Video Lectures

- **Video 1 (03:44): Module Overview** — Different AI techniques across multiple domains (machine learning, deep learning).
- **Video 2A (04:54): Unsupervised Machine Learning** — Role of unsupervised learning; K-means clustering and Gaussian Mixture Models to identify patterns in unlabeled data.
- **Video 2B (10:58): Unsupervised Machine Learning — Examples** — How unsupervised learning enhances efficiency and decision-making by uncovering hidden data patterns.
- **Video 3 (04:11): Supervised Learning Methods — Part 1** — Key aspects and applications of supervised learning; significance for informed business decisions.
- **Video 4 (09:26): Supervised Learning Methods — Part 2** — Linear and logistic regression; tree-based algorithms and neural networks.
- **Video 5 (08:25): Supervised Learning Examples — Part One** — LinkedIn's AI-powered neural networks for personalized content recommendations; Chateau Winery's AI-driven pricing strategies; AI in pharmaceutical drug discovery; Genentech's collaboration with GNS Healthcare on cancer treatment identification.
- **Video 6 (06:19): ML in Discovery of Biomarkers** — ML in pharmaceuticals: biomarkers, patient diagnostics, drug demand forecasting from historical sales and environmental data.
- **Video 7A (11:15): Algorithmic Trading and ML in Airlines and Aviation** — AI/ML in algorithmic trading, credit scoring, and aviation operations.
- **Video 7B (04:46): AI and ML in Fraud Detection and Price Optimisation** — Real-time data analysis to prevent fraudulent transactions and optimise pricing in the financial sector.
- **Video 8 (04:45): AI and ML in Wealth and HR Management** — AI/ML use in wealth management and human resource management.
- **Video 9 (02:18): Preventive Maintenance** — AI in predictive maintenance across aerospace, manufacturing, and energy.
- **Video 10 (03:43): Module Summary** — Supervised, unsupervised and reinforcement learning; role of deep learning with unstructured data; basic understanding of algorithms.
