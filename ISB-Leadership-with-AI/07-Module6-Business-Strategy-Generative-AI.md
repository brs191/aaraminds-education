### [Assignment] Required Assignment 6.2: Leveraging Generative AI in Marketing or Entertainment

**Points:** 20 | **Due:** May 27, 2026 (11:59 PM IST) | **Submission:** online upload | **Time allowed:** two weeks

**Learning outcome addressed:** Develop a comprehensive understanding of business models and strategies for monetising generative AI.

**Instructions:** Select either the marketing or entertainment industry to explore the impact of generative AI through research, interviews, and analysis.

**Part 1 — Industry Analysis:**
- *Marketing:* Pick one area where GenAI has impact (e.g. ad copy generation, social media). Research 1–2 Indian companies (examples given: WATConsult — AI for personalised campaigns and customer engagement; Mad Street Den — AI for consumer insights and personalised shopping). Write a report covering the marketing industry overview and challenges in India, specific GenAI applications, benefits and drawbacks, and future trends.
- *Entertainment:* Pick a specific area (content creation, music composition, game development, etc.). Research how GenAI is used in India's entertainment industry (examples given: T-Series — AI music composition and audience analysis; Red Chillies VFX — AI in visual effects and film production). Write a report covering the same dimensions.

**Part 2 — Professional Interviews:** Prepare questions covering implementation challenges, ethical considerations, and impact on work. Conduct at least one interview with an industry professional (or use online resources/articles/case studies as substitutes). Then use a GenAI tool (e.g. ChatGPT) with detailed prompts to simulate interviews with professionals in various roles, and compare real vs. GenAI-generated responses. (See interview guide — `files/6241535`.)

**Part 3 — Comprehensive Report:** Combine industry analysis, professional interviews, and GenAI role-play into one report including: an introduction to the selected industry and its significance; detailed analysis of GenAI applications; insights from interviews; insights from the GenAI role-play; a comparison/analysis of real vs. GenAI-generated responses; and conclusions and future outlook.

**Submission:** Upload a Word document → Start Assignment → upload file → Submit Assignment.
