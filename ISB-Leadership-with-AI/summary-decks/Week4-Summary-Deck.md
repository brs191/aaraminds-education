Understanding Pinduoduo: A Case Study in E-commerce Disruption: Part 2
--- Page 61 ---
Business Models - 5V's Model — Value Segment (segment targeted), Value Proposition (benefits divided by price), Value Creation (linked with value segment and proposition), Value Chain (process of value delivery), Value Capture (value appropriation strategy).
--- Page 62 ---
Business Model of PDD - Gamification — Value Segment: price-sensitive segment, customers in tier 3-5 cities in China, predominantly women. Value Proposition: low-quality products at low prices with minimal customer service; social interaction and steep discounts. Value Creation: emphasises category management, limited assortment and aggregation of demand through group purchases and low-cost brand building. Value Chain: shifts a traditional B2C model to C2B, using data and AI to predict demand, connecting farmers directly to end customers. Value Capture: market-based pricing strategy; steeper discounts for larger groups; strong category management and demand aggregation enable quantity discounts.
--- Page 63 ---
Business Model of Taobao — Competitive-based pricing strategy, achieving economies of scale and scope but with fewer quantity discounts. Focuses on quality products with prices matching quality, offering personalised customer service. Relies on category management and limited demand aggregation, with less emphasis on influencers.
