## Capstone Component Project Overview *(Page)*

**Overview** — Over the 36 weeks, you create your own implementation plan for technology and innovation acceleration that leverages your leadership and technology skills for your organization. If you are not currently employed or your organization is unsuitable, you may select another organization (inform your learning facilitator). Use weekly office hours to discuss ideas.

**Purpose** — Apply and implement what you learn to your job/workplace, accelerating your skills as a technology leader and possibly developing new value-creating innovations.

**Milestones** — Four required milestone assignments build the final capstone submission. Each milestone is ~one page and follows this structure: Action register (ideas to implement/investigate); Idea (select one and explain organizational support); Problem to solve; Impact (who benefits); Performance improvement (why it improves performance); Application (how you would apply it); Skill building (skills you have and additional skills needed).

**Capstone Pitch** — The final task is a pitch to implement one idea from Pillars 1–4, prepared as slides for your stakeholders (board, supervisor, department, supplier, etc.), delivered with recorded audio narration or a written script.

**Deliverables Overview:**

- **Pillar 1 — Critical Thinking (Weeks 1–3):** After the Critical Thinking modules, select one specific practice you learned and explain how it can lead to specified performance improvements. Apply it to a high-impact decision-making/problem-solving situation. Submit in Week 3.
- **Pillar 2 — System Thinking (Weeks 4–8):** After the System Thinking modules, apply one major topic to your job or organization at a systems level. Submit in Week 8.
- **Pillar 3 — Emerging Technologies (Weeks 9–15):** After the Emerging Technologies modules, select one technology and describe how to apply it creatively. Submit in Week 15.
- **Pillar 4 — Radical Innovation (Weeks 16–18):** After the Radical Innovation modules, consider a bold or breakthrough idea to apply. Submit in Week 18.
- **Pillars 5 & 6 — Organizational Strategy & Leadership (Weeks 19–23):** Create an integrated project building on one idea from Pillars 1–4. The pitch addresses: how the idea aligns with broader organizational strategy; the team that will develop and implement it; how team effectiveness is defined and lessons harvested; how to lead network ties and cultural practices to reinforce long-term continuity. Submit the presentation in Week 24.
