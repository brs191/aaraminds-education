## Instructions

In this assignment, you'll read a real-life scenario about a supervisor's request to transition to a new content management system (CMS) that is based on emotion rather than logic. You'll analyze the situation and construct an argument.

**Scenario:** Imagine you are working for a manufacturer that specializes in biomedical solutions that improve health outcomes. For months, you've been leading a team that was tasked with researching a new content management system. With the nature of your position and your organization, finding the right system was critical to both internal and external stakeholders. After you come close to reaching a decision on which content management system to select, your senior director learns which CMS your organization's direct competitor is using. In order to research the competitor's system, he's asked you to put a pause on the CMS you want to select. The problem is that you've already ruled out this CMS as a candidate due to its cost and its lack of customization and data-gathering abilities. He brushes aside your comments and asks you to take a second look.

In 400 to 500 words, construct an argument for why the competitor's CMS is not the ideal choice. Use the argument strategies presented in this module's videos.
