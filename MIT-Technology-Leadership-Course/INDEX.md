# MIT xPRO — Advanced Program in Technology Leadership & Innovation

Crawled archive of the Emeritus classroom course (course ID 16614, March 2026 cohort), captured May 25, 2026. This is a personal study archive of content the enrolled participant has access to; course materials are the intellectual property of MIT xPRO / Emeritus and this copy is for personal, non-commercial study use only.

## Scope of this archive

The course runs nine months (March–December 2026) across six pillars. As of the crawl date, **five content modules are released** (Pillars I and II); later pillars unlock on a weekly schedule and were not yet available to capture. This archive contains everything published so far.

## What each module file contains

For every module item: lesson/page text, video descriptions, quiz and knowledge-check details, assignment prompts and rubrics, discussion prompts, workbook reflection prompts, and resource lists.

## Notes

- **Video transcripts** were extracted from the module "Summary and Video Transcripts" PDF files and are stored in the `Transcripts/` subfolder.
- **Summary Decks** (PDF slide decks, one per module) are also attached to each module's resources page; these were not converted (they are slide-image PDFs) — they can be downloaded directly from the course if needed.
- **Quiz questions**: knowledge checks and surveys show their type, point value, and instructions; individual question text is not exposed by the platform until/unless attempted.
- Links and image references in the source pages were stripped (they are session-bound and would not resolve outside the platform).
