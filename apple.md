### First 30 days — Learn and diagnose

- Understand Apple Applied ML’s current platform landscape.
- Meet engineering leads, product stakeholders, infra teams, data teams, and dependent business teams.
- Review Search / IR architecture, Knowledge Graph systems, inference services, data pipelines, operational metrics, and incident history.

### First 60 days — Align strategy and execution model

- Define platform health metrics across latency, relevance, uptime, cost, throughput, model quality, defect leakage, and incident rate.
- Identify modernization opportunities across legacy search, hybrid search, vector retrieval, RAG, and inference pipelines.
- Build a roadmap with clear trade-offs across reliability, relevance, cost, speed, privacy, and governance.
- Clarify engineering ownership, decision forums, review cadence, and escalation paths.

### First 90 days — Deliver visible outcomes

- Deliver one or two measurable improvements such as latency reduction, relevance-quality instrumentation, observability, evaluation pipeline, or operational readiness improvements.
- Establish engineering governance around design reviews, release readiness, incident learning, model evaluation, and platform quality.
- Identify talent gaps and leadership bench opportunities.
- Present a 6–12 month platform strategy with measurable outcomes.
