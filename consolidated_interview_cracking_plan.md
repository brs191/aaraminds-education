## 3. Your Reusable Interview Architecture

Every answer should follow this Director-level structure:

1. **One-line executive answer**
2. **Business context**
3. **Your ownership**
4. **Key decisions and trade-offs**
5. **Execution model**
6. **Measurable impact**
7. **Leadership lesson**

Do not start with tool names, internal platform names, or long technical detail. Start with outcome, scope, and judgment.

## 12. 30/60/90-Day Plan Template

### First 30 Days — Learn, Diagnose, Build Trust

- Understand business objectives, platform scope, customer/user journeys, and success metrics.
- Meet engineering, product, architecture, operations, security, data, and business stakeholders.
- Review team structure, ownership boundaries, roadmap, incident history, cost profile, and delivery metrics.
- Identify top risks and top opportunities.
- Avoid premature reorganization or solutioning.

**Output:** Current-state assessment and top 5 priorities.

### Days 31–60 — Align, Prioritize, Stabilize

- Define measurable platform, delivery, AI, or business outcomes.
- Establish operating cadence: architecture review, delivery review, incident review, talent review, risk review.
- Prioritize 2–3 high-leverage initiatives.
- Clarify ownership, escalation paths, and decision forums.
- Start quick wins in observability, automation, cost, delivery, or AI adoption.

**Output:** Prioritized execution roadmap with metrics.

### Days 61–90 — Deliver Visible Traction

- Deliver one measurable operational improvement.
- Deliver one modernization, AI, automation, or reliability improvement.
- Establish leadership bench and talent development cadence.
- Build 6–12 month roadmap.
- Present executive dashboard and recommendations.

**Output:** Early wins plus longer-term strategy.
