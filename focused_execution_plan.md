**Candidate:** Raja Shekar Bollam · **Date:** 21 June 2026
**Why this exists:** Your plans cover ~17 roles and treat prep as an 8-week study course with applications in week 7. That over-scopes effort and assumes a pipeline you don't have. This plan does three things: pick one identity, cut to four targets, and run outreach in parallel from day one.

---

## 1. Pick ONE primary identity

Applying to Google AI Strategy + Apple Applied ML + LPL Head of Tech makes you look unfocused to any recruiter who sees more than one. Choose a spine; let everything else be an overlay.

**Recommended primary identity:**
> AI-transformation-meets-platform-engineering leader — I modernize high-scale platforms and convert AI from POCs into governed, measurable enterprise adoption, leading global teams from India.

This is where your *recent, real* work actually sits, and it stretches naturally across the four targets below. Every resume version and opening line should ladder up to this one sentence.

## 3. Run outreach in PARALLEL from week 1

The single biggest fix. Interviews schedule on the company's clock — often days after applying. Don't finish "prep" with an empty pipeline.

**Every week, regardless of study progress:**
- Apply / refresh applications for the 4 core targets (re-check JD status first — several may have moved).
- Land 2–3 referral conversations (LinkedIn, Director/Principal level, ask for guidance first).
- Log every touch: company, role, JD link, referral, applied date, response, stage, next action.

**Outreach line (reuse):**
> Exploring Director-level platform engineering and AI-transformation roles in Hyderabad. Background: large-scale platform modernization, AI-assisted engineering adoption, cloud cost optimization, global delivery. Saw [role] — strong fit around [platform modernization / AI adoption / GCC capability]. Open to a short chat or referral guidance?

## 5. Weekly cadence (replaces the 8-week course)

Run this loop continuously, not as fixed weeks — it flexes around whatever books.

| Block | Time/wk | What |
|---|---|---|
| Pipeline (apply + referrals + tracking) | 2h | Always on, from week 1 |
| Metrics + story tightening | 2h | Fill the metrics worksheet first; lock 8 STAR stories |
| Priority-1 topic study | 3h | The five topics above |
| Mock / answer-out-loud | 2h | Record yourself; cut length, add metrics, state ownership |
| Booked-interview overlay | 1h+ | Only the company that's actually scheduled |

If an interview is on the calendar, flip to: 50% that company's mocks + overlay, 30% metrics/stories, 20% pipeline.
