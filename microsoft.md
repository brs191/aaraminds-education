- Launch a structured improvement roadmap.
- Define measurable goals for engineering productivity, platform reliability, AI adoption, cost optimization, and customer value.
- Develop leadership depth inside the team.
- Institutionalize governance through dashboards, architecture reviews, operational reviews, and executive updates.
